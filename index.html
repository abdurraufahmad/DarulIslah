The Shama'il of Imam Tirmidhi

The sublime characteristics of the Prophet (SAW). A detailed commentary of selected chapters.

  • Last 10 nights of Ramadhan
  • Starting Monday 29th July 2012
  • After Taraweh Salah - 11:45pm
  • Approximately 1 hour each session

The Shama'il was compiled by the eminent Hadith scholar, Imam Abu 'Isa al-Tirmidhi less than three centuries after the passing away of the Prophet (SAW). It contains narrations dealing with all spheres of the Prophet's (SAW) life from his honourable outer appearance and characteristics to his blessed inner traits. This is an in-depth 10 night's course covering the virtue and noble character of the Prophet (SAW).
