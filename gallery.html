About

We currently have 14 classes and cater for approximately 300 students, both boys and girls. All girls' classes are segregated and take place on the 1st floor, taught by qualified Alimahs. Boy's classes take place on the ground floor, taught by qualified Alims and Huffaz. In addition, there is an adult's program on the weekends for males, and weekdays for females, where Tajweed and Islamic education is taught. Other efforts, including a daily circle to inspire the qualities of Imaan and Salah regularly take place.
